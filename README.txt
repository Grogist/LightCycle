Installation:
  Extract the zip file.
  To play, execute LightCycle.exe found in the release folder.

Overview:
The objective to LightCycle to to create walls behind you as you move and to avoid running into walls. You can either host a game or connect to another game of up to four humanplayers. Each player will also be making walls, so the playing area can become hardtonavigate fairly quickly. The last player alive wins the round.

Controls:
 Mouse:
  Moving the mouse rotates the camera around you.

Keyboard:
  W, S - moves you forward and backward.
  A, D - causes you to move left or right. Also finishes a wall that is in progress.
  R - The first press causes you to start creating a wall. The second causes you to finish the wall.
  Esc brings up the menu.
